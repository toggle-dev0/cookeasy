Classic Pancakes

20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Step 1

In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

Step 2

In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract.

Step 3

Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.

Step 4

Heat a pan over medium heat and grease lightly with butter.

Step 5

Pour small amounts of batter to form pancakes and cook until bubbles appear.

Step 6

Flip and cook the other side for about a minute.

Step 7

Serve warm with syrup or fruit.
