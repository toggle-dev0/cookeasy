Honey Garlic Salmon with Rice

25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 cup cooked white or brown rice
  • 1/2 cup steamed green beans
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)

Step 1

In a small bowl, mix honey, soy sauce, and minced garlic.

Step 2

Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.

Step 3

Cook salmon fillets for 3–4 minutes on each side.

Step 4

Pour the honey garlic sauce over the salmon and let it caramelize for 2 minutes.

Step 5

Serve the salmon over rice with steamed green beans on the side.
