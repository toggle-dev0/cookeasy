Grilled Chicken with Garlic Butter Vegetables

30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Serve the grilled chicken with the garlic butter vegetables.

Step 1

Preheat the grill or a pan over medium heat.

Step 2

Brush chicken breasts with olive oil and season with paprika, salt, pepper, and garlic powder.

Step 3

Grill chicken for 6–7 minutes on each side until fully cooked.

Step 4

In a separate pan, melt butter and sauté garlic for 1 minute.

Step 5

Grill chicken for 6–7 minutes on each side until fully cooked.

Step 6

Add broccoli and bell peppers, sauté for 5 minutes until tender.

Step 7

Serve the grilled chicken with the garlic butter vegetables.
