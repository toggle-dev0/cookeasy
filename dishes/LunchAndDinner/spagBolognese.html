Spaghetti Bolognese

40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 onion (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 250g ground beef
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Step 1

Cook spaghetti according to package instructions, then drain.

Step 2

Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté onions and garlic until fragrant.

Step 3

Add ground beef and cook until browned.

Step 4

Stir in diced tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.

Step 5

Simmer for 15–20 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Step 6

Serve the sauce over spaghetti and top with grated Parmesan cheese.
