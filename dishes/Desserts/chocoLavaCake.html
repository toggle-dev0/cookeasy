Chocolate Lava Cake

25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 4 oz dark chocolate (chopped)
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Butter and cocoa powder (for greasing)

Step 1

Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C) and grease ramekins with butter and cocoa powder.

Step 2

Melt butter and dark chocolate together, then let cool slightly.

Step 3

In a bowl, whisk eggs, egg yolks, and sugar until light and fluffy.

Step 4

Add melted chocolate, vanilla extract, and flour, mixing gently.

Step 5

Pour batter into ramekins and bake for 10–12 minutes until edges are set but the center is gooey.

Step 5

Let cool for 1 minute, then carefully invert onto a plate and serve warm.
