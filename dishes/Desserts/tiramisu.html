Classic Tiramisu

30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee (cooled)
  • 2 tbsp coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 8 oz mascarpone cheese
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 pack ladyfinger biscuits
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder (for dusting)

Step 1

Mix brewed coffee with coffee liqueur and set aside.

Step 2

In a bowl, whisk egg yolks and sugar over a double boiler until thickened.

Step 3

Remove from heat and mix in mascarpone cheese until smooth.

Step 4

Whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form, then fold into the mascarpone mixture.

Step 5

Dip ladyfingers into the coffee mixture and layer them in a dish.

Step 5

Spread a layer of mascarpone cream over the ladyfingers and repeat the layering.

Step 5

Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before dusting with cocoa powder and serving.
