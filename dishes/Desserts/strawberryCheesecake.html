Strawberry Cheesecake Parfait

15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup crushed graham crackers
  • 8 oz cream cheese (softened)
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup fresh strawberries (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp honey

Step 1

In a bowl, mix cream cheese, sugar, Greek yogurt, and vanilla extract until smooth.

Step 2

In serving glasses, layer crushed graham crackers at the bottom.

Step 3

Add a layer of the cheesecake mixture, followed by fresh strawberries.

Step 4

Repeat layers and drizzle honey on top.

Step 5

Serve immediately or refrigerate for 30 minutes before enjoying.
