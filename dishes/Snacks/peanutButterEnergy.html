Peanut Butter Banana Energy Bites

10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana (mashed)
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds (optional)

Step 1

In a bowl, mix mashed banana, peanut butter, honey, cinnamon, and chia seeds.

Step 2

Stir in rolled oats until well combined.

Step 3

Roll into small bite-sized balls and place on a plate.

Step 4

Refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving for a firmer texture.
