Spicy Roasted Chickpeas

30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 8 oz mascarpone cheese
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (optional)

Step 1

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

Step 2

Pat chickpeas dry with a paper towel to remove excess moisture.

Step 3

In a bowl, toss chickpeas with olive oil and spices.

Step 4

Spread evenly on the baking sheet and bake for 25–30 minutes, shaking halfway through.

Step 5

Let cool slightly before serving for a crispy texture.

Step 5

Spread a layer of mascarpone cream over the ladyfingers and repeat the layering.
